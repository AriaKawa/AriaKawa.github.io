Base vs. Swapped Pair

Base sentence

私は図書館に行きます。

Focus: topic frame (as for me) + destination point.

Swapped sentence

私が図書館に行きます。

Focus: emphasis on who is going (I’m the one).

Prompt: Describe the Meaning Shift

Prompt: In one or two sentences, describe how the focus changes when は becomes が.

Hint: does the sentence feel like a topic statement or a contrast/emphasis?

Model Explanation & Paraphrase

Model explanation: は frames the topic (“as for me”), while が highlights the subject as new or contrastive information (“I am the one who is going”).

Paraphrase (topic): 私について言うと、図書館に行きます。 Paraphrase (emphasis): 行くのは私です。
